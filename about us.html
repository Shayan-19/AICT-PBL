    Our Story

    Founded by a team of passionate explorers and travel enthusiasts, Aurora North Expeditions was born out of a deep love for the northern areas of Pakistan. From the towering peaks of the Karakoram to the lush valleys of Swat, we have dedicated ourselves to providing travelers with an authentic, immersive experience in one of the most stunning and remote regions of the world. Our mission is simple: to offer a gateway to the breathtaking beauty and vibrant culture of northern Pakistan, while ensuring sustainable travel practices that protect these fragile environments.

    What We Offer

    At Aurora North Expeditions, we offer a wide range of tailored tours and expeditions that cater to all kinds of adventurers. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, we provide:
    Northern Lights Tours: Though northern Pakistan may not have the Northern Lights, we offer incredible night sky views in the high-altitude regions, with stargazing sessions and an opportunity to witness the beauty of the Milky Way.
    Wildlife Safaris: Discover the incredible wildlife of northern Pakistan, from the elusive snow leopard to wild ibex and the majestic markhor. Our expert wildlife guides lead safaris through remote regions like Khunjerab National Park, the Kaghan Valley, and more.
    Winter Expeditions: Explore the winter beauty of the northern valleys, including the stunning landscapes of Hunza, Swat, and Skardu. Enjoy activities such as snow trekking, ice climbing, and snowshoeing.
    Cultural Immersion: Get up close to the rich culture of the region. Visit traditional villages in Hunza, Gilgit, and beyond, and experience local art, music, and cuisine. Learn about the centuries-old traditions that make the northern areas of Pakistan so unique.
    Custom Expeditions: Whether you're looking for an adventurous trek, a wildlife safari, or a cultural immersion, we create custom itineraries to match your interests and needs.
