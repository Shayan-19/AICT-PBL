About Us

Welcome to Aurora North Expeditions, your gateway to the captivating wonders of the northern landscapes. We are passionate explorers dedicated to curating unforgettable travel experiences, showcasing the untouched beauty, vibrant cultures, and thrilling adventures the North has to offer.


Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire wanderlust while fostering a deep connection to nature and the communities we visit. Whether you're chasing the Northern Lights, trekking through majestic mountains, or immersing yourself in local traditions, Arora North Expeditions ensures every journey is meaningful, sustainable, and truly unforgettable.
Join us in discovering the magic of the North—where every expedition is a story waiting to unfold.


Our Promise

Aurora North Expeditions is more than a tour operator—it’s a promise of adventure, connection, and awe. We invite you to join us on a journey to the Arctic’s wonders, where you’ll create memories that last a lifetime.



Featured Tours


Winter Wonderland Adventure
Embark on a multi-day journey through the breathtaking, snow-capped peaks and frozen lakes of Pakistan's northern regions. Experience the magic of the Northern Lights as you explore the serene valleys of Hunza, Swat, and Gilgit, and traverse snow-covered paths on a thrilling trek or snowshoeing adventure. Perfect for winter enthusiasts, this tour offers a chance to immerse yourself in the raw beauty of the Karakoram and Himalayan ranges during the winter season.
Northern Pakistan Wildlife Discovery
Venture into the remote wilderness of northern Pakistan and discover the region’s rich wildlife. From the elusive snow leopard to graceful ibex and wild goats, our expert guides will take you on an unforgettable safari through the forests, valleys, and high-altitude meadows. Explore the stunning landscapes of Kaghan Valley, Khunjerab National Park, and more as you encounter the diverse wildlife that calls these mountains home.
Northern Lights & Culture Tour
Combine the mesmerizing beauty of the Northern Lights with the vibrant culture of Pakistan’s northern communities. Visit the iconic villages of Hunza, Gilgit, and Skardu, and engage with the indigenous cultures, rich in history and tradition. Learn about local craftsmanship, music, and stories passed down through generations. This tour offers a perfect blend of natural wonder and cultural immersion in one of the most stunning regions of the world.
